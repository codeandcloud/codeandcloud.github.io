--- layout: post title: Prevent site from getting loaded on iframe date: '2010-11-16T10:16:00.004+05:30' author: Naveen categories: [obslete] tags: [obslete, javascript, iframe] modified_time: '2010-11-16T11:14:10.561+05:30' --- What if somebody loads your obfuscated fancy javascript or AJAX methods to drive traffic to their site utilising your bandwidth? Here is a simple solution in javascript.

<script type="text/javascript">
//<![CDATA[
    if (window.top !== window.self) {
        document.write = "";
        window.top.location = window.self.location;
        setTimeout(function () {
            document.body.innerHTML = '';
        }, 1);
        window.self.onload = function (evt) {
            document.body.innerHTML = '';
        }; 
    }
//]]>
</script>


Paste that javascript snippet inside your head tag of your html.
